GEN-Z FOR CHANGE INITIATIVE

Gen-Z for Change Initiative is a national youth-led advocacy organization that educates and activates masses of young people to become involved in progressive organizing. We build effective digital tools with powerful advocacy in areas such as labor, climate, and immigrant justice, and leverage our extensive network of 500+ content creators and organizing partners to mobilize Gen-Z audiences across the country.

SOME OF OUR INITIATIVES

We educate Gen-Z on issues ranging from climate change to reproductive justice and direct audiences to ways they can directly be part of the solution using our digital advocacy tools. By building simple, effective tools and fostering strong relationships with creators, we’re dedicated to educating and empowering our generation in the fight to preserve our democracy.

Creator Supper Club

In 2025, we launched Creator Supper Club, a monthly dinner series bringing together creators and organizers from our network to build community and create content around one pressing area of advocacy. Thus far, we’ve been able to catalyze change with millions of views, bringing attention to AI-powered surveillance, supporting striking Starbucks workers, and platforming the stories of immigrants in detention.

Eyes on AI

This year, Gen-Z for Change launched eyeson-ai.org/: a mass education campaign dedicated to bringing awareness to the AI-powered surveillance apparatus through an innovative new tool. The Eyes on AI tool prompts you to detail the ways in which you are subjected to surveillance in your everyday life and provides you with material interventions to reduce your individual level of risk.

Meddle Media

In 2025, Gen-Z for Change launched Meddle Media: a long-form content wing of the organization, dedicated to providing fact-based educational content on civic and social issues. We are committed to countering misinformation and disinformation pipelines through long-form content curated and produced by trusted creators for young audiences.
